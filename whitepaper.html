1. THESIS

The internet forgets fast. Memes are born, go viral, and disappear without context. Archive of Meme ($ARCH) turns the community into curators of the canon: every day we nominate, vote, and archive an immutable "Meme Capsule".

💡 One-liner: "Your humor today, history forever."

2. PRODUCT OVERVIEW

Daily Cycle:

📝
Nominations (00:00–16:00 UTC)
Submit a meme with metadata (image/link/author/license/context)
🗳️
Voting (16:00–20:00 UTC)
$ARCH holders vote for their favorites
🏆
Archive (20:00 UTC)
TOP 3 MEMES sealed in Daily Capsule with unique URL

Weekly Canon: Every Sunday, 1 meme from the week enters the permanent Canon through special community vote.

Credentials: Earn soulbound badges - Nominator, Curator, Canon-Keeper - for your contributions.

3. WHY IT MATTERS

  • Cultural: Preserves key pieces of internet history with proper context and credit
  • Social: On-chain status earned through good curation, not just token holdings
  • Technological: Open metadata + public API for builders and researchers
  • Memetic: Simple daily ritual creates high retention and community engagement

4. TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE

Core Components:

  • Network: Solana blockchain for speed and low cost
  • Storage: Images and metadata on Arweave/IPFS for permanence
  • Smart Contract: Minimalist on-chain program

Data Structure:

DailyCapsule { date: YYYY-MM-DD, arweave_tx: hash, merkle_root: hash, meme_count: 3 } WeeklyCanon { week_id: number, capsule_ref: hash, meme_id: string }

Public API Endpoints:

  • /capsules/latest - Today's capsule
  • /capsules/{date} - Historical capsule
  • /canon - Weekly canon entries
  • /leaderboard - Top curators

6. ARCHIVE RULES & ETHICS

Content Guidelines:

  • Permissions: Priority to CC0/own content/explicit permission
  • Transformation: Parody and transformative memes accepted
  • Attribution: Original creators always credited when known
  • Prohibited: No pornography, doxxing, or hate speech

Takedown Process:

Legitimate copyright holders can request content removal. Public mirror removed, on-chain record marked as "revoked" for transparency.

7. GOVERNANCE

Phase 1 (Launch):

  • Treasury multisig (3/5 signatures)
  • Signal voting via snapshot
  • Core team operational decisions

Phase 2 (Decentralized):

  • AIP (Archive Improvement Proposal) system
  • Quorum: ≥5% of active voters (30-day window)
  • Time-locked execution

8. SECURITY & RISKS

Technical Security:

  • Upload sanitization and size limits
  • Rate limiting per wallet
  • Signature verification for votes
  • Redundant storage (IPFS + Arweave)

Risks:

⚠️ IMPORTANT RISKS:
  • Extreme price volatility - you can lose everything
  • Smart contract bugs possible despite audits
  • Regulatory uncertainty in some jurisdictions
  • Platform adoption may never materialize
